LATTICERUNNER

24.0 — CSIS TABLETOP WARGAME
A turn-based strategic wargame calibrated to the CSIS "First Battle of the Next War" (2023) findings. Each turn = one day of a Chinese amphibious invasion of Taiwan. You command US/Taiwan forces with limited Command Points per turn.

COMMAND POINTS limit your actions each day — prioritize wisely. Harden defenses, lay mines, stage LRASM, commit reserves, open diplomatic channels. Posture toggles are free. Special ops cost CP.

CSIS TARGET: ~73-80% survival with Japan basing. ~35-45% restricted. ~12-20% denied.
