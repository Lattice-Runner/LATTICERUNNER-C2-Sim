LATTICERUNNER

24.0 — CSIS TABLETOP WARGAME
A turn-based strategic wargame calibrated to the CSIS "First Battle of the Next War" (2023) findings. Each turn represents one day of a Chinese amphibious invasion of Taiwan. You command US/Taiwan forces. Make policy decisions, allocate forces, and resolve combat. Japan basing access is the single largest variable. The PLA AI adapts to your strategy. Victory requires surviving 10 days. Defeat occurs if the Presidential Office falls.
